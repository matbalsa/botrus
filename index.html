    About Us

    Botrus Sports Hub was created to make managing sports clubs and associations easy and hassle-free. We understand that grassroots sports associations and clubs are predominantly run by volunteers, which is why our platform is designed to be simple, time-efficient, and intuitive.

    Our platform connects players, parents, coaches, and local businesses into a vibrant community hub. From game scheduling to video highlights, we provide all the tools needed to create an engaging sports community.

    Join us in building a stronger, more connected sports community!
